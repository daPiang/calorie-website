CALORIES

Why count calories?

Counting calories can help you lose weight by bringing awareness to what you eat each day. This can help you identify eating patterns that you may need to modify, keeping you on track to reach your goals.

Activity Levels

Sedentary: no exercise/workouts
Light: workouts 1-3 times/week
Moderate: workouts 4-5 times/week
Active: daily workouts or intense workouts 3-4 days/week
Very Active: intense workouts 6-7 days/week
Extra Active: intense workouts daily/physical labour
